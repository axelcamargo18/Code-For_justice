4 Everybody

Our Foundation works to investigate, prevent, intervene and understand a series of health and social problems that especially affect groups at risk or social exclusion. Our goal is to improve people's quality of life and promote social inclusion through the promotion of health and collective well-being.

Our Vission

As a foundation, we are dedicated to quality care for people in difficult situations and social exclusion, helping them to exercise their social rights and improve their quality of life. We have a clear commitment to equal opportunities between men and women.

Our Mission

Promote the health, social welfare, and quality of life of people in situations of social exclusion and their environment through the development of social intervention, prevention, awareness, education, and research programs.
